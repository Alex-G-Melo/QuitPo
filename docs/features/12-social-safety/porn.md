# Social Media Safety - Pornography Addiction

This file contains pornography addiction-specific social media safety configurations. See [README.md](README.md) for shared functionality.

## Focus

Configure platforms to block/reduce:
- NSFW content
- Suggestive/sexual imagery
- Adult content creators
- Explicit DMs and spam
- Algorithm-driven suggestive content

## Platform Guides

### Instagram Safety Guide (6 Steps)

**Step 1: Enable Sensitive Content Control**
1. Go to Profile → Three lines (☰) → Settings and privacy
2. Scroll to "Suggested content"
3. Tap "Sensitive content"
4. Select "Less" (strictest option)

*This limits sensitive content in Explore and Reels.*

**Step 2: Clear Your Algorithm**
1. Settings and privacy → Your activity
2. Select "Time spent"
3. Tap "Clear Instagram recommendation history"

*This resets what Instagram thinks you want to see.*

**Step 3: Hide Suggested Content**
1. When you see triggering content in Explore/Reels
2. Tap the three dots (⋮)
3. Select "Not interested"
4. Repeat consistently for a few days

*Instagram learns to stop showing similar content.*

**Step 4: Restrict Story Replies**
1. Settings and privacy → Messages and story replies
2. Under "Story replies," select "People you follow" or "Off"

**Step 5: Filter DM Requests**
1. Settings and privacy → Messages and story replies
2. Enable "Filter offensive words and phrases"
3. Under "Message requests," set to "People you follow"

**Step 6: Additional Recommendations**
- Unfollow accounts that post suggestive content
- Use mute instead of following borderline accounts
- Avoid the Explore tab entirely
- Consider making account private

### TikTok Safety Guide (6 Steps)

**Step 1: Enable Restricted Mode**
1. Profile → Three lines (☰) → Settings and privacy
2. Go to "Content preferences"
3. Enable "Restricted Mode"
4. Set a passcode you'll forget (write it in Alex Friend notes)

*Cannot be easily disabled once set with passcode.*

**Step 2: Filter Video Keywords**
1. Settings and privacy → Content preferences
2. Select "Filter video keywords"
3. Add triggering words: sexy, hot, bikini, thirst trap, babe, etc.

**Step 3: Clear Watch History**
1. Settings and privacy → Activity center/Watch history
2. Delete all watch history
3. Consider doing this weekly

**Step 4: Use "Not Interested"**
1. On any triggering video, long press
2. Select "Not interested"
3. Choose "Hide videos from this creator"
4. Do immediately - don't watch the video

**Step 5: Refresh For You Feed**
1. Settings and privacy → Content preferences
2. Select "Refresh your For You feed"

*Completely resets recommendations.*

**Step 6: Enable Screen Time Limit**
1. Settings and privacy → Digital wellbeing
2. Enable "Daily screen time limit"
3. Set to 30-60 minutes max

### Twitter/X Safety Guide (5 Steps)

**Step 1: Enable Safe Search**
1. Profile icon → Settings and privacy
2. Privacy and safety → Content you see
3. Enable "Hide sensitive content"

**Step 2: Block Sensitive Media**
1. Settings → Privacy and safety → Content you see
2. Disable "Display media that may contain sensitive content"

**Step 3: Mute Keywords**
1. Settings → Privacy and safety
2. Mute and block → Muted words
3. Add: nsfw, 18+, onlyfans, sexy, nude, lingerie, etc.

**Step 4: Protect DMs**
1. Settings → Privacy and safety → Direct messages
2. Enable "Filter low-quality messages"
3. Consider disabling DMs from non-followers

**Step 5: Curate Timeline**
- Unfollow triggering accounts
- Use Lists for news (bypasses algorithm)
- Turn off "Show the best Tweets first"
- Avoid Explore tab

### YouTube Safety Guide (5 Steps)

**Step 1: Enable Restricted Mode**
1. Profile picture → Settings → General
2. Enable "Restricted Mode"

**Step 2: Clear Watch History**
1. Settings → History and privacy
2. Tap "Clear watch history"
3. Consider "Pause watch history"

**Step 3: Clear Search History**
1. Settings → History and privacy
2. Tap "Clear search history"
3. Consider "Pause search history"

**Step 4: Remove Recommendations**
1. On triggering thumbnails → Three dots (⋮)
2. Select "Don't recommend channel" or "Not interested"

**Step 5: Use Subscriptions Tab**
1. Use Subscriptions instead of Home
2. Only shows channels you chose
3. Avoids algorithmic recommendations

**Note on Shorts**: YouTube Shorts is harder to control. Swipe past triggering content immediately and tap "Not interested."

### Reddit Safety Guide (5 Steps)

**Step 1: Disable NSFW Content**
1. Profile → Settings → Content Preferences
2. Disable "Show NSFW content"
3. Enable "Blur NSFW images" (backup)

**Step 2: Leave NSFW Subreddits**
1. Review subscribed subreddits
2. Leave any with triggering content
3. Many innocent-seeming subreddits can be triggers

**Step 3: Disable Personalized Recommendations**
1. Settings → Account Settings
2. Disable "Personalize recommendations based on your activity"

**Step 4: Use Old Reddit (Desktop)**
1. Access via old.reddit.com
2. Better content controls
3. Less algorithmic, more chronological

**Step 5: Avoid r/all and r/popular**
- These can surface NSFW despite settings
- Stick to subscribed feed
- Consider third-party apps with better filtering

### Snapchat Safety Guide (4 Steps)

**Step 1: Hide Sensitive Content**
1. Profile → Settings (⚙️)
2. Privacy Controls → Sensitive Content Controls
3. Select "Less"

**Step 2: Control Discover/Spotlight**
1. Tap and hold on triggering content
2. Select "See less like this"
3. Disable Spotlight notifications

**Step 3: Limit Who Can Contact You**
1. Settings → Privacy Controls
2. Contact Me → "My Friends" only
3. View My Story → "My Friends" only

**Step 4: Clear Conversations**
1. Settings → Account Actions
2. Clear Conversations with triggers

**Pro Tip**: Stay on Chat tab, avoid Discover/Spotlight entirely.

---

### Discord Safety Guide (4 Steps)

**Step 1: Enable Explicit Content Filter**
1. User Settings (⚙️) → Privacy & Safety
2. Explicit Content Filter → "Keep me safe" (scans all)

**Step 2: Limit DMs**
1. Privacy & Safety
2. Disable "Allow direct messages from server members"
3. Enable "Enable message requests from server members"

**Step 3: Leave NSFW Servers**
1. Review all servers
2. Leave any with NSFW channels
3. Many gaming servers have hidden NSFW areas

**Step 4: Disable Server Discovery**
1. Settings → Privacy & Safety
2. Disable suggestions and recommendations

## Priority Order

For pornography addiction, prioritize configuring:

1. **Browsers** (via Content Blocker) - Primary gateway
2. **Reddit** - Heavy NSFW presence
3. **Twitter/X** - Easy NSFW access
4. **Instagram** - Suggestive content in algorithm
5. **TikTok** - Suggestive content in FYP
6. **Discord** - NSFW servers and DMs
7. **Snapchat** - Discover section, direct messages
8. **YouTube** - Thumbnails and recommendations

## After Configuration: Ongoing Maintenance

### Weekly Actions
- Use "Not interested" on any triggering content
- Review and remove new problematic follows
- Check DM settings still enabled

### Monthly Actions
- Clear algorithm/history on worst platforms
- Review any new accounts added
- Re-check settings haven't reset

### If Triggered Despite Settings
- Note which platform and content type
- Add to custom block list (if available)
- Consider deleting the app entirely
