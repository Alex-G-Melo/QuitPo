## Moderation: Trigger Words

Auto-flag posts containing:
- Specific game names in glorifying context
- Gaming session invites
- Discord/gaming platform links
- Discussing gaming achievements proudly
- "Just one game" justifications

Auto-block:
- Gaming platform invites
- Server/guild recruitment
- External links to gaming content
- Streaming links

## Community Guidelines (Gaming-Specific)

### Encouraged Content
- Sharing balanced living victories
- Discussing real-world hobbies
- Celebrating physical activities
- Asking for offline activity suggestions
- Supporting others through withdrawal boredom

### Not Allowed
- Gaming session invitations
- Guild/clan recruitment
- Glorifying gaming achievements
- "Moderate gaming is fine" promotion
- Streaming or gaming content links

### Gray Area (Moderator Discretion)
- Discussing why specific games were addictive - allowed if educational
- Career in gaming industry - allowed if about balance
- Esports watching without playing - moderator discretion
