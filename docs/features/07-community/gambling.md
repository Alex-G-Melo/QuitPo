## Moderation: Trigger Words

Auto-flag posts containing:
- Specific betting site names
- Betting tips or predictions
- Requests for loans
- Detailed gambling strategies
- Sports betting odds discussion

Auto-block:
- External links (especially to betting sites)
- Requests for money
- Gambling promotion
- Personal financial details

## Community Guidelines (Gambling-Specific)

### Encouraged Content
- Sharing days without gambling
- Discussing money management techniques
- Celebrating financial milestones
- Discussing self-exclusion experiences
- Supporting others through tough events (payday, sports)

### Not Allowed
- Sharing betting tips or predictions
- Discussing odds or "sure things"
- Glorifying past wins
- Requesting or offering loans
- Promoting gambling as entertainment

### Gray Area (Moderator Discretion)
- Discussing debt amounts - allowed if recovery-focused
- Sports discussion without betting context - allowed
- Financial recovery strategies - allowed if not gambling-related
