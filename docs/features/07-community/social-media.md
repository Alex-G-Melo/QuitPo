## Moderation: Trigger Words

Auto-flag posts containing:
- Links to social media content
- "Follow me" or handle sharing
- Viral content discussion that encourages checking
- Competition about followers/likes
- Promoting social media use

Auto-block:
- External links (especially to social platforms)
- Social media handles
- Image sharing attempts
- Engagement baiting

## Community Guidelines (Social Media-Specific)

### Encouraged Content
- Sharing screen time reductions
- Discussing presence techniques
- Celebrating phone-free activities
- Recommending offline hobbies
- Discussing attention span recovery

### Not Allowed
- Sharing social media handles
- Discussing viral content
- Promoting "good" social media use
- Follower/like competitions
- Linking to social posts

### Gray Area (Moderator Discretion)
- Discussing news events - allowed if focused on avoiding doom-scrolling
- Work-related social media - allowed if about boundaries
- Dating app discussion - allowed if about healthy limits
